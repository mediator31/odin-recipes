Quiche

Description

This is the best recipe of Spinach Quiche you can make

Ingredient

  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Muenster Cheese
  • Spices(Salt and peper)

How To make the desired quiche

  1. Cook the vegetables
  2. Whisk the eggs and seasonings, then stir in the cheese and spinach mixture.
  3. Pour the mixture into a pie pan and bake until the eggs are cooked through and set.
